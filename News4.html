FOUR CENTENARIANS FROM URDANETA AND ALCALA, RECOGNIZED BY CAPITOLYO AND FIRST SPOUSES LEAGUE OF PANGASINAN



Four centenarians in Urdaneta and Alcala were personally visited by the Provincial Social Welfare and Development Office (PSWDO) and the First Spouses League of Pangasinan under the leadership of First Lady Maan Tuazon-Guico this May 11.

In the program "Honoring Our Pangasinense Centenarian" of the provincial government, the First Lady handed the certificate of recognition.

The centenarians are Juliana Eugenio Bugaon from barangay Nancamaliran West and Victoria Tabor Eulalio of Brgy. Camanang, Urdaneta City, Federico Carbonnel Villon and Maria Untalan Estacio from Brgy. St. Nicholas, Alcala. They were also given food packs, hygiene kits and P20,000 cash.

Former Urdaneta City Councilor Jesus 'Isong' Basco, Alcala Mayor Jojo B. Callejo, San Jose Barangay President Bartolome Palagud, Nancamaliran West Kgwd. Mike Mendoza, Pastor Peter Cabael of San Nicolas, Alcala, and PSWDO staff. (Marilyn Marcial, Joey Olimpol RED)
