Hundred Island Festival

The Hundred Island Festival is an annual celebration held in the Province of Pangasinan, known for its beautiful islands and captivating charm. It offers a range of activities and events that showcase the unique beauty and cultural heritage of the region. From the Hundred Island Festival itself to the Miss Hundred Island pageant, the Light Parade and Street Dancing Competition, the Alaminian Grandball, the Ginebra Fest, and the Talaba Ihaw Ihaw culinary experience, visitors can immerse themselves in a vibrant and diverse celebration. The festival provides an opportunity to experience the stunning islands, witness the grace and elegance of the pageant contestants, enjoy colorful displays and lively performances, revel in an evening of sophistication and grandeur, partake in exciting entertainment and Ginebra-inspired drinks, and savor the mouthwatering flavors of fresh oysters. The Hundred Island Festival promises a captivating and memorable experience for all attendees.
