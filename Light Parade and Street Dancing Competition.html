Light Parade and Street Dancing Competition

Tonight’s event is a dazzling celebration of rich cultures, foods and traditions, creativity and diversity, the feast of our patron saints and the bountiful harvest and the lives of the locales of western Pangasinan, as the contingents coming from different schools in our district, illuminate the streets with their vibrant colors and electrifying performances. This annual Light Parade and Street Dancing Competition is an event like no other, where participants showcase their stunning festival costumes while dancing to the rhythm of their festival music.

In his message, Mayor Arth Bryan C. Celeste congratulated and thanked all the participating schools that include St. Nicolas Catholic School of Anda, Barlo Integrated School of Mabini, Don Marcelo Jimenez Memorial Polytechnic Institute, Inc. of Dasol, Inerangan National High School, Alaminos City National High School and Pangasinan State University – Alaminos City Campus. He likewise expressed the city`s profound gratitude to the sponsors, the organizing committee, and all the guests and visitors who joined the celebration.

Alaminos City National High School (ACHNS) emerged as this year’s grand champion and won a whooping P350,000. ACNHS also bested other competitors in the Best in Choreography and Light Effects category. Meanwhile, the representative of Alaminos City National High School was adjudged as the Festival Queen 2024 with P15,000.00 as cash prize and trip to Puerto Princesa, Palawan for two (2) as additional incentive. While Inerangan National High School bagged home the first runner-up and received P250,000. Pangasinan State University ended up as 2nd runner-up which goes with P150,000 cash prize. Congratulations to all our winners and our heartfelt gratitude to our sponsors! Event Chairpersons: Katherine Camba and Junaliza Bravo
