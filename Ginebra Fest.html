Ginuman Fest 2024

The annual Ginebra Festival here in our city never fails to entertain and draw a huge and appreciative crowd. And this year’s music fest is no exception as thousands of Alaminians and guests flocked at Lucap Park to enjoy a whole night of exhilarating music, exciting games and surprises, and lots of drinks and shared memories with friends and families.

Performing live was Sponge Cola, one of the popular rock bands in the country, and featured acts from rapper Kiyo, Avant Music and Hiraya Band.

Mayor Arth Bryan Celeste extended the city government`s sincerest gratitude and appreciation to Ginebra San Miguel, Inc. for bringing anew the Ginuman Fest here in our city as part of the Hundred Islands Festival 2024. Along with the top executives of GSM Inc. headed by National Sales Manager of GSMI Manny P. Factor, Area Sales Manager Joel R. Caluya, Trade Promo & Merchandising Manager Ms. Dianne S. Justiniano, Territory Sales Manager Jay R Mangalino, City Mayor Arth Bryan C. Celeste, Event Chairman Maridel Rabac and other city officials, led the ceremonial toast to signal the start of the grand celebration of music, friendship, good food, drinks and surroundings and most especially, our resilience as Filipinos as highlighted by new Ginebra San Miguel’s ad campaign “Lamang ang May Tapang”.

Also in attendance manifesting full support to the activities of the Hundred Islands Fest 2024 are 1st District Congressman Arthur F. Celeste with his wife Ms. Chloue Celeste, Ms. Caroline Gaweco- Celeste the wife of Board Member Arthur Celeste Jr., City Vice Mayor Jan Marionne Fontelera, City Councilors, City Administrator Atty. Walter Macaiba, LGU Department Heads and Section Chiefs and MHI 2024 Queens. Maraming Salamat po Boss Ramon S. Ang, GM Noli Macalalag and to the whole GSMI-family!!!
