Welcome to Alaminos City

Welcome to Alaminos City

A progressive and vibrant community driven by a shared vision of growth and development. As the local government, we extend a warm and sincere welcome to our webpage, where you can explore the many facets of our remarkable city. Discover valuable information about our public services, from healthcare and education to transportation and infrastructure, ensuring the well-being and convenience of our residents and visitors.

Immerse yourself in our rich history and cultural heritage, embraced by the warmth and hospitality of our residents who make Alaminos City a true home away from home. Furthermore, explore the economic opportunities and entrepreneurial spirit that thrive here, supported by a conducive environment for growth and innovation.Beyond administrative matters, Alaminos City is blessed with natural wonders that will leave you in awe.

The Hundred Islands, a true marvel of nature, beckon adventurers and nature enthusiasts to explore their beauty and immerse themselves in the wonders of the sea. Engage in exciting activities such as snorkeling, diving, island-hopping, and beachcombing, offering endless opportunities for outdoor exploration and rejuvenation. Our webpage serves as a transparent platform for us to connect with you, providing the latest news, events, and developments in Alaminos City.


Join us in shaping the bright future of our city, whether you are a resident, a visitor, or a potential investor.

Together, let's build a stronger and more inclusive community in Alaminos City.

Mission

A pro-active City Government, setting standards in good governance, serving as an architect of holistic development, an integrator of development initiatives and provider of opportunities to the broadest segments of its constituents.

Vision

We envision Alaminos City by 2030 to be a major growth center and prime tourism hub in Region 1 propelled by a sustainable, competitive and progressive local economy, with adequate, appropriate and world-class facilities within a conserved and ecologically-balanced environment, which are inhabited by a healthy, God-loving and educated citizens, and served by transparent, united and responsive local government leaders.
