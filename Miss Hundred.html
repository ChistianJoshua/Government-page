Miss Hundred Island

Exuding confidence, showcasing beauty, grace, and wit, and promoting her noble advocacy, the new Miss Hundred Islands was officially crowned at the just-concluded Grand Coronation Night at the New Lucap Park. Representing their beloved towns and cities, our 22 lovely candidates conquered the stage as they vied for the coveted crown as well as the new role as the City’s Tourism Ambassador.

Crowned as the Miss Hundred Islands 2024 was Ms. Denise Joy C. Flores of Lingayen.

Together with her reign are Miss Hundred Islands-Tourism 2024 Reisha Mae F. Valdez from the town of Umingan, Miss Hundred Islands-Environment 2024 Kyrie Shane L. Aragon of Urdaneta City, Pangasinan, 1st Runner Up Jessalyn S. Espinoza from the town of Mangaldan, and 2nd Runner-up April Mae M. Flores also from the town of Lingayen.

Interestingly, our newly crowned queens start their journey in time for the celebration of our International Women’s Month, where they are expected to bring the spotlight on empowered women, of substance and of greater purpose. City Mayor Arth Bryan C. Celeste warmly welcomed everyone and congratulated our new queens. He also extended his heartfelt gratitude to the event chairperson, Board Member Arthur C. Celeste, Jr. and his wife Caroline, co-chair CDRRMO Mylene O. Manalastas as well as the major and minor sponsors and to AIKA Productions headed by Joemel Ramos.

1st District Congressman Arthur F. Celeste and his lovely wife, Madam Chloue C. Celeste also graced the event along with Department of Agrarian Reform (DAR) Undersecretary - Field Operations Officer Atty. Kazel C. Celeste, who also served as one of the esteemed judges.

Also in attendance were League of Municipalities of the Philippines – Pangasinan Chapter President and Municipal Mayor of Lingayen Leopoldo Bataoil, City Vice Mayor Jan Marionne Fontelera, City Administrator & Hundred Islands Festival Executive Chairman, Atty. Walter M. Macaiba, and other city officials and Miss Hundred Islands 2023 Monica Valle and her court.

Other members of the star-studded panel of judges were Gines Enriquez, Hanna Arnolds, Angelica Lopez, Eric Phan, Elika Bunter, Cindy Dionisio, Pauline Amelinckx, and Matthaois.

TV actor and model Marco Gumabao and Miss Globe 2021 Maureen Montagne served as the pageant hosts. The newly crowned Miss Hundred Islands and her court held a press conference at the city tourism office right after the coronation.

Congratulations and good luck on your reign, Queens, and make our province proud!
