STUDENTS GIVEN EDUCATIONAL ASSISTANCE WITH THE HELP OF SEN. IMEE MARCOS, AND OF DSWD



With the help of the Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD) and Sen. Imee Marcos, around 1,000 students in Binalonan were given Educational Assistance under DSWD Assistance to Individual in Crisis Situation (AICS). Each student received P5,000 from the said program.
Up to 4,000 students in Pangasinan from the towns of Bayambang, Binalonan, and the cities of San Carlos and Urdaneta were given financial assistance. Also with us in the program are Mayor Ramon RG Guico IV and members of the Municipal Council of Binalonan headed by Vice Mayor Bryan Louie Balangue.

Source: RAMON MON MON GUICO III
